We believe that access to data empowers people

We are a community of people who believe that an integral part of building an empowered society is providing access to data and information that allows denizens to participate in democracy.

OpenUp was started with the mission of liberating data so that people could actively engage with what is happening in society and contribute to decisions that affect their lives on a personal, local and national level.

What we learned is that just making data available is not enough. To those who need this information the most - ordinary denizens - the data is useless if they canâ€™t find it, understand it or use it.

So we started building tools that make the data more accessible: easy to understand, easy to share, easy to engage with.

We believe that with access to the right and relevant information, people are empowered to participate by adding their voice, highlighting issues that affect them and finding solutions to problems.
