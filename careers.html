Chief Operating Officer

OpenUp is a civic technology non-profit start-up based in Cape Town. We work towards promoting an open government and active citizenry through technology and open data. We work with government, civil society, media and academia to build open source tools and websites that help citizens make informed decisions. Some of the tools we have built include:

  • municipalmoney.gov.za
  • www.wazimap.co.za
  • www.youthexplorer.org.za
  • openbylaws.org.za
  • mpr.code4sa.org

OpenUp is seeking a mission-focused, seasoned, strategic, and process-minded leader with experience scaling an organisation, working as part of an executive management team, and developing a performance culture among a group of diverse, talented individuals. The COO must be a leader who is able to help others at OpenUp deliver measurable, cost-effective results that realise the organisation’s vision. While it is essential that the COO bring efficient and effective systems to increase the productivity of the organisation, is it also critical that the team retain the creative passion that drives OpenUp.

Duration: Full time, permanent role
Salary: Commensurate with years of experience
Starting date: Immediately

Responsibilities

Reporting to the Director of OpenUp, the COO will lead all internal operations and will have the following responsibilities:

  • Actively participate in the Senior Management Team, championing our strategic goal of building a world-class civic technology organisation.
  • Work in partnership with the Director to create the strategic five-year plan and implement new processes and approaches to achieve it.
  • Work with other members of the Senior Management Team to ensure high quality delivery of internal functions as well as donor/grant reporting.
  • Design and implement business strategies, plans and procedures.
  • Lead the performance management process that measures and evaluates progress against goals for the organization.
  • Ensure the smooth day-to-day operations of the organisation.
  • Evaluate organisational risks to ensure that OpenUp makes informed decisions across the various elements of organisational risk.
  • Oversee and coordinate financial and organisational planning with the Chief Financial Officer, including annual budgets, consistent with OpenUp’s strategic directions and values.
  • Oversee financial investments and assets, managing expansion activities and relationships with partners/suppliers.
  • Ensure efficient use of all OpenUp resources and undertake regular cost-benefit analysis to inform planning.
  • Ensure that all regulatory and donor requirements are met, and that OpenUp leads by example on transparency and accountability.
  • Write and submit reports to the Director in all matters of importance.
  • Coordinate reporting to Board.
  • Promote a cohesive and productive workplace

Requirements

  • At least 10 years of experience as Chief Operating Office or relevant role
  • Demonstrable competency in strategic planning and business development
  • Strong professionalism and integrity and enjoys working closely with our Director and fellow members of the Senior Management Team
  • A minimum of 5 years experience working with with civil society (having worked or volunteered in civil society organisations) and the legal, regulatory and funding environment that organisations like ours operate in.
  • Excellent writing, interpersonal and public speaking skills.
  • Excellent people skills, business acumen and exemplary work ethics.
  • Experience in fundraising will be a plus.
  • Commitment to the organisation’s mission and values.

How to apply: Apply for this position by sending an email to jobs@openup.org.za. Please attach both your CV and a cover letter.

Project Manager

OpenUp is a civic technology non-profit start-up based in Cape Town. We work towards promoting an open government and active citizenry using technology as a tool. We also liberate and democratise data so as to inform citizens to make informed decisions about their lives. We have developed a number of tools towards this goal such as:

  • www.wazimap.co.za
  • www.youthexplorer.org.za
  • openbylaws.org.za
  • mpr.code4sa.org
  • noconfidencevote.openup.org.za

We are looking for a Project Manager to support the successful delivery of our websites, tools and projects, including working with diverse stakeholders.

Activities

  • Manage technical and non-technical projects throughout their entire execution to ensure success in terms of scope, budget and timeline
  • Develop and maintain project plans through input from stakeholders
  • Manage the day-to-day execution of projects using agile methodologies like scrum
  • Run project sprints, including daily standups, backlog management and retrospectives
  • Monitor and report on project progress to stakeholders
  • Manage project risk by working with technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Collaborate with non-technical project team members, including technical members, communications and stakeholder engagement
  • Be an advocate for a good user experience

Requirements

  • Based in Cape Town
  • At least 3 years experience of project management using agile methodologies
  • Experience communicating at various levels
  • Proven track record of project delivery
  • Project management certification advantageous
  • Excellent computer skills (Google Drive and/or MS Office, project management tools, etc.)
  • Strong communication skills
  • Fluent in English
  • Ability to self-manage
  • Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively
  • Has their own laptop
  • Experience managing technical (software) projects is an advantage
  • Experience working on open source projects is an advantage
  • Non-South Africans must have a valid work permit

How to apply: Apply for this position by sending an email to jobs@openup.org.za. Please attach both your CV and a cover letter.
